# Q1 Business Review

## Executive Summary

Revenue increased across core regions, but margin pressure remains the largest risk.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value | Change |
|---|---:|---:|
| Revenue | $12.4M | +12% |
| Gross Margin | 61% | -3pp |
| Net Retention | 118% | +4pp |

## Risks

- Margin pressure from infrastructure costs.
- Enterprise pipeline concentration.
- Delayed renewals in EMEA.

## Recommendations

1. Reprice high-usage accounts.
2. Improve self-serve onboarding.
3. Expand compliance package for enterprise buyers.
